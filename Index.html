WELCOME TO MY BLOG...

My name is Hoang Anh (Arsper). I am in the early twenties. Currently, I am living in Hanoi. I have been studying Information technology at University of Greenwich (Vietnam). I am an introvert. Sometimes, my personality makes people feel unapproachable. So I don't have many friends either. However, I also have 5-6 close friends. They are wonderful people. But I also love to make new friends. About me in general: Introverted, disciplined, dynamic, fastidious, diligent... I am also a person who always pursues professionalism and perfection. My biggest hobbies are football, programming and watching movies. I usually spend my free time enjoying Hanoi's food or watching movies alone in Circle K.

During university, I tried to improve my English, even though I didn't like it. I also do web design as a hobby and also to develop my programming skills. My products are mainly small websites. It is simulated large websites or some travel websites. However, that is quite normal.

This blog was created by me in 3 days. It is completed on 30/07/2021. It's pretty simple because I don't invest much time either. The link of this blog is made public by me on my personal facebook page. So if you've visited my profile and seen this blog, you can send me a message or a friend request. That would be an honor for me.

MEMORIES

Learn to enjoy minute of your life. Be happy now.

Don’t wait for something outside of yourself to make you happy in the future.


Le Loi High School

Mon 29 Apr 2019

Le Loi High School - where I missed the best things and the best friend for me.

High school is my regret.

The time in high school was when I felt most comfortable. But now, I feel regret for that comfort. I missed a lot of things in high school. I didn't study English hard, even though English is one of my three main subjects. I was determined to become a soldier. It wasn't until the middle of my senior year that I realized I was wrong. Then I chose information technology. If I had embraced it and learned it from the beginning, I would have been able to do more in college. And most of all, I missed someone, someone special to me.

Visit FPT software. It was my first company visit when I entered college.

HOLA

Sat 14 Nov 2020

I gave up on my dream of becoming a soldier. And I tried that as a sophomore.

I tried becoming a soldier when I was a sophomore.

Wow! It was obviously a great experience as a college student. As I said, becoming a soldier is my dream. I love traveling. And I don't think the military would be the right choice. While in Hola, I lived with my classmates. The new day starts at 5:30 a.m. and ends at 9:30 p.m. Discipline is always enhanced. It took me a week to get used to it. But after 24 days in Hola, I have changed a lot. More positive. It was clearly a rewarding experience.

Marriott Hotel

Sat 30 Jan 2020

A hotel employee. This is not my major. But it gives me the experience.

I used to be a part-time hotel employee.

This is also a great experience with a friend. Although this is not my major, it also helped me understand that making money is not easy. Every day I went to work in the past, I could make new friends and do new things. That helped me learn to adapt to every new situation. I have also encountered many types of managers. Some people are funny, some people are very difficult. And I've learned to adapt to that. I also stopped this job when I was a third year student. Now, I have to spend all my time on my major.

University of Greenwich

2019 - 2023

I chose Greenwich. I don't regret that decision. Greenwich is my home.

Greenwich is my home.

This is a difficult choice. But I think I was right. Here, I do not have to study unnecessary subjects. I learned English with high quality. I learned soft skills subjects. I have visited big companies, participated in many talk shows or workshops. I get to express myself. After studying here, i have changed a lot. More mature, more confident, more disciplined, more professional, more dynamic, tougher. And most of all, Greenwich helped me know what I needed to do and what I really wanted. Thank you Greenwich. I love Greenwich. Greenwich is my youth.

Ha Noi

2019 - Present

I was born in Thanh Hoa. I love my hometown. But Hanoi is something special for me.

Hanoi is something special for me.

When I came to Hanoi in 2019, I really hated Hanoi. It was crowded and noisy. People are quite careless. My friends are still on vacation. I had to familiarize myself with a whole new place, a new school, and new friends. Hanoi makes me tired. But as a sophomore, I changed my view of Hanoi. I really fell in love with this place. I like West Lake. I love the food here. I found joy in my work with my college friends. I love the work I'm doing. I love studying at Circle K. I love walking the streets of Hanoi on winter evenings. I have a little problem with some girls. But it ended early. Hope things get better for me ^-^.
