WELCOME TO MY BLOG...

My name is Hoang Anh (Arsper). I am in the early twenties. Currently, I am living in Hanoi. I have been studying Information technology at University of Greenwich (Vietnam). During that time, I also did web design as a hobby and also to develop my programming skills. My products are mainly small web sites. It is simulated big websites like shopee or some travel websites. I have also created a few small applications using the c# programming language. However, it is quite normal.

This blog was created by me in 3 days. It is completed on 30/07/2021. It's pretty simple because I don't invest much time either. The link of this blog is made public by me on my personal facebook page. So if you've visited my profile and seen this blog, you can send me a message or a friend request.
